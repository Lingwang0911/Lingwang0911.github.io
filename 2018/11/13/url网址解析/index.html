format（urlObject):将对象格式转化为字符串
resolve(from,to)将参数对象拼接成浏览器可以识别的格式
e.g：
①url.resolve(‘/one/two/three’,’four’)//‘/one/two/four’
②url.resolve(‘http://example.com/','/one')//'http://example.com/one'
③url.resolve(‘http://example/one','/two')//'http://example.com/two'
